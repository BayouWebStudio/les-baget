Our Story

Les Ba'get Vietnamese Café originally started as a beloved food truck that captured Houston's heart with authentic Vietnamese flavors. We've now expanded to our larger location in Garden Oaks, bringing you the same incredible dishes that made us famous.

Our menu features traditional Vietnamese dishes like bánh mì sandwiches and phở, as well as more creative offerings with a unique Texan twist. Every dish is prepared with fresh ingredients and traditional techniques passed down through generations.

From our humble food truck beginnings to our current home in Garden Oaks, we remain committed to serving authentic Vietnamese cuisine with the warmth and hospitality you deserve.
